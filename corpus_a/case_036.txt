A somewhat more complicated question than the previous one.

Regarding weight, metformin to a lesser extent, SGLT-2 inhibitors and, above all, GLP-1 agonists have been associated with a significant decrease in weight in patients with DM-2 (answers 1,3 and 4 incorrect).

On the other hand, pioglitazone, as reflected in its data sheet, can produce dose-dependent weight gain, mainly due to fat accumulation and added, in some cases, to water retention.
