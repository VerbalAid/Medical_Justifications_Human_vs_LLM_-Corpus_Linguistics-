The trick to this question is that there are 8 cervical roots for 7 vertebrae, therefore the C1 root exits ABOVE the atlas, and so on up to C8 which exits BETWEEN C7 and T1.

The thoracic roots come out respectively below the vertebra with their same numbering (nerve T1 under T1, T2 under T2, etc.).

Therefore, between C4 and C5 the 5th cervical nerve exits, and between T4 and T5 the 4th thoracic nerve exits.

The correct answer is 4.
