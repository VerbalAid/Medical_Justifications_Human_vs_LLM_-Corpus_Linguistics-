It is important to have a radiological diagnosis in view of a possible local treatment, such as neurolytic treatment or plexus blockade.

He is taking WHO step 3 analgesia, which is clearly insufficient, so he will need adjuvants; although opioids for neuropathic pain are not very effective, we will not reduce the dose but will associate the adjuvants.
