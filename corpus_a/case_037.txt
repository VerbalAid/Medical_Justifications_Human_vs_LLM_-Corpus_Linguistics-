This is a typical case of cervical incompetence (and this patient has conization as a risk factor).

This pathology consists of dilatation of the cervix in the absence of contractions, requiring cerclage to prevent miscarriage or immature delivery.

The cerclage is ideally performed electively, programmed, and not when the patient arrives at the ER "in extemis" (which are also done, but with a lower success rate... the ideal is to do them cold).
